Why should you listen to Moh’s Ambient Hour?

Hand-picked songs

Moh’s Ambient Hour features a daily selection of hand-picked ambient songs for you to listen to when you’re relaxing, working, focusing, driving, or whatever you’re up to.

Spotify playlists

Moh’s Ambient Hour airs in daily Spotify playlist. That means that you won’t have to worry about missing out on an episode. You decide when what plays. Spotify playlists are also available everywhere you go – they are synced on your desktop PC, your smartphone, your SmartTV, car radio, and other devices that support Spotify playback.

#MohMission

Moh’s Ambient Hour’s #MohMission aims to bring the joy of ambient tunes and songs to everybody; to people like you and I, old veterans of the genre, and new-comers who might not even know that they like this kind of music genre. #MohMission is a perfect place to start your ambient journey as our playlists are not driven by any company to promote or push their songs. #MohMission brings you the finest in ambient music – independently chosen by our editor team.
