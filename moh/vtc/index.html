Who are the Moh Hauliers?

The ones that forward cargo in the name of Moh are usually refered to as “Moh Hauliers”. A squad of elite drivers who make sure that every single job meets company standards and comes to their clients without any damage.

In the name of Moh.

The term "Moh" has stopped being just a username a long time ago. Nowadays, the name Moh stands for quality, customer contact, high precision, and excellent service. Truckers for Moh are recognizable by their blue "Moh M" which must be present on their uniform at all times.

Convoy Security Force

Moh Hauliers Convoy Defense Task-Force will make sure that any convoy leaving one of the Moh Delivery Centre bases will arrive at its destination without any loss or damage. Protecting Moh Haulier convoys are squad of specially trained, ex-militray MP officers who have received exsessive training in close-quarter combat as well as lethal and non-lethal weapon training.

Why should I report a driver?

Moh Hauliers wants to provide its clients with the highest in service, performace, and quality. Sometimes, drivers do not meet these conditions. If you choose to report a driver, you help us to improve our fleet.

So, why should I report any misconduct I see?

  1. Help us to find out what additional training the driver needs. If one of the drivers shows any type of misconduct, we will make sure to give them the opportunity to seek extra training to make sure their work can be executed with maximum efficiency.
  2. Help us to fire those who are not willing to respect company policy. Sometimes, drivers decide to go—as we call it—rogue. This means that a driver that has previously shown to be a valuable addition to our team, no longer respects our conditions and therefore starts to put the company's reputation down. We will make sure that these drivers will be punished and, if their behaviour continues, be expelled from the company itself.
